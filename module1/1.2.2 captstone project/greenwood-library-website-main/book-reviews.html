Featured Reviews

The Midnight Library

★★★★★

by Matt Haig

An extraordinary book about the choices that go into a life well lived. The protagonist, Nora Seed, finds herself in a library between life and death where each book represents a different version of what her life could have been.

- Reviewed by Morgan

Project Hail Mary

★★★★☆

by Andy Weir

A thrilling science fiction novel about an astronaut who wakes up in a spaceship with no memory of how he got there. The story is filled with scientific problem-solving and an unexpected friendship.

- Reviewed by Jamie

Educated

★★★★★

by Tara Westover

A powerful memoir about the author's journey from growing up in a survivalist family in Idaho to earning a PhD from Cambridge University. A testament to the transformative power of education.

- Reviewed by Alex
