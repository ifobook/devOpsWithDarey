Our Story

Founded in 1985, Greenwood Library has been serving the community for over 35 years. What started as a small collection of books has grown into a vibrant community center for learning and discovery.

Our Mission

To provide free and open access to information, ideas, and knowledge through books, media, and programs that educate, enrich, and empower our community.

Our Team

Our dedicated staff and volunteers are passionate about connecting people with information and fostering a love of reading and learning in our community.
